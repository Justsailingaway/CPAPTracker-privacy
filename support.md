# CPAP Tracker Support

## Need Help?

We're here to help you get the most out of CPAP Tracker. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to reach us.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is CPAP Tracker?**
CPAP Tracker helps you manage your CPAP equipment by tracking replacement schedules, cleaning tasks, and optionally viewing sleep data from Apple Health.

**Is my data stored online?**
No. All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

### Equipment Tracking

**How do I add a new consumable?**
Go to the Consumables tab and tap the "+" button. Select the type of consumable and set a replacement interval.

**What does "Due Soon" and "Overdue" mean?**
- **Due Soon**: The consumable is within 20% of its replacement date.
- **Overdue**: The replacement date has passed. Tap the consumable to replace it.

**Can I track more than 5 consumables?**
The free version supports up to 5 consumables. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited tracking.

### Sleep Data

**How do I see my sleep data?**
Go to the Sleep tab. The app reads sleep data from Apple Health. You'll need to grant HealthKit permission when prompted.

**What if I don't have sleep data?**
You can preview the feature using example data. Go to Settings and look for the "Example Sleep Data" section.

**Why isn't my sleep data showing?**
Make sure HealthKit access is enabled: go to iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > CPAP Tracker and enable sleep data access.

### Cleaning

**How does the cleaning schedule work?**
The Cleaning tab shows daily, weekly, and monthly tasks based on the consumables you're tracking. Tap a task to mark it complete.

### Pro Upgrade

**What does Pro include?**
- Unlimited consumables
- Ad-free experience
- Replacement history
- All future Pro features

**Is Pro a subscription?**
No. Pro is a one-time purchase with lifetime access.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Go to Settings > tap "Upgrade to Pro" > tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the paywall.

### Notifications

**How do I set up reminders?**
The app will ask for notification permission on first launch. You can manage notifications in iOS Settings > Notifications > CPAP Tracker.
