# Privacy Policy for CPAP Tracker

**Last updated: January 19, 2025**

## Overview

CPAP Tracker ("the App") is designed to help you manage your CPAP equipment replacement and cleaning schedules. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally
The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to external servers:
- CPAP consumable replacement dates and schedules
- Cleaning task completion records
- Notification preferences
- App settings

### Health Data
If you choose to enable HealthKit integration, the App can read sleep analysis data from Apple Health. This data:
- Is accessed only with your explicit permission
- Is displayed within the App for your personal use
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is never shared with third parties

### Advertising Data
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data for ad personalization

This information is collected by Google and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How We Use Your Data

- **Local data**: To provide app functionality, track your equipment schedules, and send you reminder notifications
- **Health data**: To display your sleep information within the app (if you grant permission)
- **Advertising data**: To serve relevant advertisements and support free access to the app

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob** - For displaying advertisements ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Apple HealthKit** - For accessing sleep data (governed by Apple's privacy practices)

## Your Choices

### Advertising
You can opt out of personalized advertising:
- Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
- Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

You can also reset your Advertising Identifier:
- Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
- Tap "Reset Advertising Identifier"

### Health Data
You can revoke HealthKit access at any time:
- Go to iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → CPAP Tracker
- Disable access

### Notifications
You can disable notifications:
- Within the app's Settings
- Or via iOS Settings → Notifications → CPAP Tracker

## Data Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We do not have access to your data.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
